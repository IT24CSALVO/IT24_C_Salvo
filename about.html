About

Victoria’s Secret is an American lingerie, clothing, and beauty retailer that has become one of the most iconic and recognizable brands globally, particularly known for its bras, panties, lingerie, and beauty products. Founded in 1977 by Roy Raymond in San Francisco, the brand was initially conceived as a place where men could feel comfortable buying lingerie for women. It soon evolved into a go-to destination for women seeking stylish, sensual, and trendy lingerie.
